Nice to meet you!

My name is Alexander. I am a curious Interaction designer who enjoys creating playful experiences. I was born and raised near Helsingborg, in a small town in the south of Sweden and have been fascinated by people and technology my entire life.

I have studied electrical engineering at Lunds University, before I realised that a big part of my other passion was being left out, which is psychology and human interactions. I have now graduated from Malmö’s University with a degree in Interaction design. Right now I'm looking for a job or internship where I can put my skills and knowledge as an interaction designer to work. I’m also really looking forward towards learning new things and growing as an interaction designer.

If you have seen the media page on this website, you might have picked up on that I'm fascinated about visual communication. I love coming up with creative ways to express a message* . Sometimes that message is deeper and sometimes it’s about one particular aesthetic expression and other times I’m learning new things.

When I'm not designing, you can find me researching various subjects, watching films, practicing guitar or listening to music. If you want to come in contact with me use the links below. I am always interested in meeting new people.
